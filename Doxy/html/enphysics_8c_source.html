249// setLimit(), a few notes:
250// _softness:
251// 0->1, recommend ~0.8->1.
252// describes % of limits where movement is free.
253// beyond this softness %, the limit is gradually enforced until the "hard" (1.0) limit is reached.
254// _biasFactor:
255// 0->1?, recommend 0.3 +/-0.3 or so.
256// strength with which constraint resists zeroth order (angular, not angular velocity) limit violation.
257// __relaxationFactor:
258// 0->1, recommend to stay near 1.
259// the lower the value, the less the constraint will fight velocities which violate the angular limits.
